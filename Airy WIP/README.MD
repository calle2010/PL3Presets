# Airy

This preset softens the image and colors and fade to white add the edges.
This may be applicable to high key image of people or still life.

- Preset Type: Partial

- Requires: 
  - DxO PhotoLab 3 Elite
  - FilmPack 5 Essential

- Included Tools:
  - Exposure correction
  - Contrast
  - DxO ClearView (off)
  - DxO Smart Lighting
  - Color accentuation
  - Unsharp Mask
  - Tone curve
  - Creative vignetting
